Lasagna

A delicious Italian pasta classic with many iterations filled with meat, a Bechamel sauce and pasta, topped with enough cheese to kill you several times over.

Ingredients
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 26 oz. Pasta sauce
  • 2 Cloves of garlic; minced
  • 15 oz. Ricotta cheese
  • 10 oz. Spinach
  • 3.5 oz. Pesto
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup Grated stringy cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Black Pepper to taste; freshly ground
  1. Prehead oven to 350
  2. Cook noodles according to package; drain.
  3. Spread 2 cups of the pasta sauce in the bottom of a greased 13 x 9 inch baking dish.
  4. Combine garlic, ricotta, spinach, pesto, egg, Parmesan, salt and pepper to taste in a bowl.
  5. Place lasagna noodle on a clean kitchen towel; spread 1/4 cup of the ricotta mixture on the noodle.
  6. Roll noodle up; place seam side down in pan. Repeat with remaining noodles.
  7. Pour remaining pasta sauce over noodles; sprinkle with mozzarella.
  8. Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes
  9. Uncover; cook until cheese is golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes longer.
